<Seasonal Picking>
Once, Zhuang Zhou dreamed he was a butterfly, a butterfly flitting and fluttering about, happy with himself and doing as he pleased. He didn't know that he was Zhuang Zhou.

Suddenly he woke up and there he was, solid and unmistakable Zhuang Zhou. But he didn't know if he was Zhuang Zhou who had dreamt he was a butterfly, or a butterfly dreaming that he was Zhuang Zhou. Between Zhuang Zhou and the butterfly there must be some distinction! This is called the Transformation of Things.

...

Zhuangzi, chapter 2
(Watson translation)

Grower's Guide

<Call for Artists>
Local Produce is a grass roots creative communication curated by Unholycow—an entity that seeks to cultivate a field of udderly chaotic ideas.

Local Produce is a completely organic venture. We offer independent creators an ideal compost to plant the seeds of their imagination, within the spaces of our community garden. If you would like to see an articulation of yours feature in our next curative harvest, here's what you need to do:
  1. Grow your original genius around the genus of our seasonal picking (refer above). Your ideas must be your own and envisage the theme embodied by the prompt in one of three forms: visual, aural or scribal.

  2. Submit your visual (.jpg/.png), aural (.flac/.wav) or scribal (.docx/.txt) contribution via email to moo@unholycow.in with your name and locality. You may choose to use a pseudonym; Unholycow respects your right not to be known.

  3. Acknowledge that the fruits of your labour won't be commercialised. Unholycow is content without capital intent. All our curative efforts carry and will culminate in the Creative Commons license {CC BY-NC-SA}. We ask that you make peace with its terms prior to your submission.

  4. Sit back and relax. If we sense what we like, we'll reach out to you. All contributions are considered. So, we are sometimes overwhelmed by the volume of produce. Recognise that the harvest is a seasonal venture: if not by this moon, your work may well fruit in the next. Yet, if it’s absence is prolonged and you remain entirely un-notified: we apologise here for the oversight.
Godspeed!
